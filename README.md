# Walmart Sales Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing Walmart's retail sales performance across multiple dimensions including customer segments, product categories, geographical locations, and temporal trends for the year 2023. This dashboard provides end-to-end sales analytics for Walmart's home improvement and construction product lines, offering insights into customer behavior, product performance, discount strategies, and store-level metrics to drive strategic business decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators

### Overall Metrics (2023)
- **Total Sales:** 1.90M
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 7,529 units
- **Average Discount:** 3.67%
- **Date Range:** January 1, 2023 - December 31, 2023

## Sales Performance Analysis

### Monthly Sales Trends
Sales remained relatively stable throughout 2023 with notable patterns:
- **Peak Month:** December (370K) - holiday season surge
- **Strongest Quarter:** Q1 (Jan-Mar: ~340K average)
- **Weakest Month:** November (260K)
- **Average Monthly Sales:** 316K

### Discounted vs. Regular Sales
Monthly breakdown shows consistent discount strategy:
- Average discounted sales: ~150K per month
- Discount penetration: ~47% of total sales
- Highest discounted sales: January-March (170K)
- Lowest discounted sales: June (130K)

## Customer Segmentation

The dashboard tracks four distinct customer segments:
- **DIY Homeowner** - Individual home improvement enthusiasts
- **Professional Contractor** - Trade professionals and builders
- **Property Manager** - Commercial property maintenance
- **Renovation Enthusiast** - Home renovation specialists

## Business Insights

### Revenue Optimization
- December shows 42% increase over November - capitalize on Q4
- Q1 maintains strong performance - focus marketing efforts
- Summer months (Jun-Aug) show decline - consider promotional campaigns

### Product Strategy
- Power tools and hardware drive revenue - expand inventory
- Building materials underperform - review pricing/positioning
- Top 5 products account for ~400K (21% of total sales)

### Discount Effectiveness
- 3.67% average discount maintains healthy margins
- ~50% discount penetration balances acquisition and profitability
- Discounted sales remain consistent - indicates effective pricing strategy

### Geographic Focus
- Seattle, LA, Chicago dominate - allocate resources accordingly
- Top 3 stores likely drive 40-50% of total revenue
- Opportunity to analyze underperforming locations
